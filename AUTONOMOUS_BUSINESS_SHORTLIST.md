# Autonomous Business Shortlist

## Purpose

Select business candidates that fit the CEO's real goal:

```text
Build an AI company that can operate primarily by AI.
Human role is final approval only.
```

## Excluded Models

The following are excluded as main businesses:

- sales agency
- DM outreach business
- consulting
- custom manual diagnosis
- bespoke research service
- labor-heavy agency work

`Japan Readiness Audit` is no longer treated as the main business.

It can remain as a learning asset, but it does not fit the CEO's core objective because acquisition and delivery are too human-dependent.

## Evaluation Criteria

Each candidate is evaluated on:

1. Can AI operate it?
2. Is the human role limited to final approval?
3. Is sales dependency low?
4. Can it scale to JPY 1M+/month?
5. Can it become SaaS?
6. Can it become an API?
7. Can it sell automatically?

## 1. Cross-Border Store Trust Scanner

### What It Is

A self-serve SaaS/API that scans ecommerce stores and identifies trust gaps that stop international buyers from purchasing.

Japan can be the first scoring module, but the business is not limited to Japan.

### Why It Fits

- URL-in, score-out product.
- AI can perform most analysis.
- Human only reviews edge cases.
- Can become SaaS, API, and Shopify app.
- Can sell through SEO, app marketplaces, and freemium scans.

### Evaluation

| Item | Rating |
|---|---|
| Human dependency rate | 10-20% |
| AI dependency rate | 80-90% |
| JPY 1M difficulty | Medium |
| Entry barrier | Medium |
| Required capital | Low-medium |
| MVP period | 2-4 weeks |

### Revenue Path

- Free scan
- $29-$99 detailed report
- $29-$79/month monitoring
- $199/month agency plan
- API usage pricing

### Main Risk

Demand wording. Merchants may not search for "trust scanner."

### Mitigation

Use practical positioning:

```text
Find what stops international buyers from trusting your store.
```

## 2. AI Policy & Compliance Copy Checker For Ecommerce

### What It Is

A tool that checks store policy pages and flags unclear or risky buyer-facing language:

- shipping policy
- return policy
- refund policy
- duties/taxes wording
- support expectations
- delivery claims

It does not provide legal advice. It checks clarity, completeness, and buyer confidence.

### Why It Fits

Policy pages are public, text-heavy, and highly automatable.

### Evaluation

| Item | Rating |
|---|---|
| Human dependency rate | 10-20% |
| AI dependency rate | 80-90% |
| JPY 1M difficulty | Medium |
| Entry barrier | Medium |
| Required capital | Low |
| MVP period | 2-3 weeks |

### Revenue Path

- $19 one-time policy check
- $49/month policy monitoring
- $149/month multi-store agency plan
- API for ecommerce agencies

### Main Risk

Legal-advice boundary.

### Mitigation

Position as:

```text
policy clarity checker
```

not:

```text
legal compliance checker
```
