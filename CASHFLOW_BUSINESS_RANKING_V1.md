# Cashflow Business Ranking V1

## Purpose

Keep `Revenue Risk Scanner` as the main business.

Add a separate cashflow line to protect owner living expenses within 90 days.

This is not a replacement for the main business.

## Constraints

Cashflow candidates must fit:

- AI-led
- human only gives final approval
- operable on MacBook Pro 2017
- low monthly fixed cost
- possible revenue within 90 days
- does not compete heavily with Revenue Risk Scanner development resources

## Evaluation Items

Each candidate is evaluated on:

1. time to first revenue
2. AI automation rate
3. difficulty to reach JPY 100k/month
4. difficulty to reach JPY 300k/month
5. difficulty to reach JPY 1M/month
6. synergy with Revenue Risk Scanner

Difficulty scale:

```text
Low = easier
Medium = realistic but requires execution
High = difficult
Very High = unlikely within short period
```

## Recommended Cashflow Strategy

### Best First Cashflow Offer

```text
Ecommerce Policy Fix Pack
```

Why:

- closest to Revenue Risk Scanner
- fast to deliver with AI
- narrow enough to productize
- creates reusable templates
- low fixed cost
- can sell before full scanner is ready

### Second Offer

```text
Shopify Store FAQ Gap Pack
```

Why:

- simple
- high automation
- easy to turn into scanner module

### Third Offer

```text
Revenue Risk Mini Audit PDF
```

Why:

- strongest synergy
- validates scanner output
- but must be kept tightly scoped to avoid becoming consulting

## What Not To Do

Do not start:

- custom consulting
- DM sales agency
- broad copywriting service
- manual market research
- anything requiring new hardware
- anything requiring high monthly fixed cost
