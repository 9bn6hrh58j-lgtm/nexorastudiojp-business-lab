# High Value Lead Market Map V1

## Purpose

Adopt `SUCCESS_FEE_BUSINESS_MAP_V1.md` and move to the next phase.

The question is no longer:

```text
Who pays 10,000 JPY+ per lead?
```

The new question is:

```text
Who pays 10,000 JPY+ per lead, and can those leads be acquired cheaply enough?
```

This document assumes:

- niche success-fee model
- no SaaS assumption
- AI as command center
- human work allowed
- profit must exceed acquisition and operation cost

## Core Rule

A high-value lead market is attractive only when:

```text
lead value - acquisition cost - qualification cost - dispute cost > profit target
```

High buyer willingness alone is not enough.

Some markets pay a lot because leads are extremely hard to acquire.

## Evaluation Axes

| Axis | Meaning |
|---|---|
| High-ticket niche | Is one customer/deal valuable enough? |
| Acquisition difficulty | Can leads be sourced cheaply? |
| Buyer count | Are there enough buyers willing to pay? |
| Lead value | Can one qualified lead command 10,000 JPY+? |
| AI-led operation | Can AI source, qualify, route, and track? |
| 150k difficulty | How hard to reach 150,000 JPY/month? |
| 1M difficulty | How hard to reach 1,000,000 JPY/month? |

Scoring:

```text
5 = favorable
4 = good
3 = mixed
2 = weak
1 = poor
```

For difficulty columns:

```text
5 = easier
1 = very hard
```

## Executive Judgment

The best early niche is not the niche with the highest lead price.

The best early niche is:

```text
high enough lead price + reachable buyers + cheap intent capture + simple qualification
```

Top starting candidates:

1. Small business website / landing page redesign leads
2. Local commercial cleaning quote leads
3. Pest control / termite inspection leads
4. Exterior painting / roof inspection leads
5. Corporate training / workshop leads
6. B2B bookkeeping / back-office service leads
7. Dental implant / orthodontic consultation leads
8. Home renovation micro-niche leads
9. Cybersecurity assessment leads for SMBs
10. Used business equipment buyer leads

Avoid as first move:

- personal injury legal
- broad real estate
- broad insurance
- enterprise SaaS demos
- M&A leads

Reason:

They may have high lead value, but competition, trust, regulation, or acquisition cost can kill margins.

## Top 10 Analysis

## 1. Small Business Website / Landing Page Redesign Leads

## Why Buyers Pay

Small businesses need websites, landing pages, and conversion improvements, but many have outdated sites.

Buyers:

- freelance web designers
- small agencies
- no-code studios
- local marketing agencies
- niche landing page builders

## Lead Value

```text
10,000-50,000 JPY per qualified project lead
```

Higher if the lead has:

- existing site
- clear problem
- budget
- timeline
- decision maker

## Acquisition Difficulty

```text
Low-medium
```

Leads can be found via:

- Google Maps businesses with bad websites
- public directories
- social media posts
- newly opened shops
- local business lists
- "website renewal" intent posts

## AI-Led Operation

AI can:

- find businesses
- detect outdated websites
- create outreach angles
- qualify need
- prepare lead briefs
- route to web agencies

## Monthly 150,000 JPY Path

```text
15 leads x 10,000 JPY
5 leads x 30,000 JPY
```

## Monthly 1,000,000 JPY Path

```text
50 leads x 20,000 JPY
20 leads x 50,000 JPY
```

## Main Risk

Low-quality leads and agency skepticism.

Must define:

```text
lead = business owner confirmed interest in redesign or landing page help
```

## Judgment

```text
GO
```

Best first test because buyer access is easy.

## Best First Validation Plan

## Step 1: Choose 3 Buyer Niches

Recommended:

1. small business web/landing page redesign
2. commercial cleaning
3. B2B bookkeeping/back-office

Reason:

- buyers are easy to identify
- no heavy regulation
- one closed customer is valuable
- lead definition can be clear
- AI can source and qualify

## Step 2: Pre-Sell Buyers Before Generating Leads

Do not generate leads first.

Ask providers:

```text
Would you pay 10,000-30,000 JPY for a qualified lead matching these criteria?
```

Define criteria:

- real need
- contact info
- geography
- timeline
- budget or willingness to discuss
- consent to be contacted

## Step 3: Run Manual/AI Lead Sourcing

Use AI for:

- list building
- signal detection
- lead scoring
- qualification forms
- personalized messages
- routing
- tracking

Human handles:

- final lead quality check
- partner communication
- payment agreement
- dispute handling
