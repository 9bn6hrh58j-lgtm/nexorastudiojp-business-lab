# AI Setup Business Evaluation V1

## Purpose

Evaluate `AI environment setup / AI orchestration support` as a short-term cashflow candidate.

This is not treated as the main business.

Main business direction remains:

```text
Revenue Risk Scanner
```

The question is:

```text
Can AI setup support produce cash quickly without distracting too much from the main product?
```

## Candidate Definitions

### Revenue Risk Scanner

A URL-only pre-audit that instantly explains why buyers may not purchase, beyond what analytics tools can explain.

Core value:

```text
3+ specific, evidence-backed, fixable findings that customers did not notice.
```

### AI Environment Setup / AI Orchestration Support

A productized setup service that helps small teams or solo operators build a working AI operations environment.

Possible scope:

- AI tool stack selection
- prompt/workflow setup
- Codex / Claude / ChatGPT workflow design
- document generation workflow
- task handoff workflow
- browser automation workflow
- local workspace structure
- lightweight agent coordination rules
- handoff templates
- safety/checklist system

This must not become open-ended consulting.

## Comparison Summary

| Item | Revenue Risk Scanner | AI Setup Support |
|---|---|---|
| Role | Main product candidate | Short-term cashflow candidate |
| First revenue | Slower until MVP output is convincing | Faster if sold as fixed package |
| Human dependency | Low-medium after productization | Medium-high unless tightly productized |
| AI sales potential | Strong | Medium-low |
| JPY 1M/month path | Product-led SaaS/App/API | Service-heavy unless packaged |
| 5-year barrier | Data, workflows, integrations | Weak unless converted to platform/templates |
| Owner fit | Strong long-term | Useful short-term, risky if it becomes consulting |

## 1. Initial Revenue Timeline

| Business | Expected First Revenue | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue Risk Scanner | 30-90 days | Needs better finding quality, sample proof, landing page, and paid unlock. |
| AI Setup Support | 7-21 days | Existing market pain is immediate; can be sold as fixed setup package. |

### Judgment

AI Setup Support is better for first cash.

However, this advantage only holds if the offer is fixed:

```text
One setup package, one intake form, one delivery template, one review call max.
```

If it becomes custom consulting, the business will consume the owner.

## 2. Human Dependency Rate

| Business | Human Dependency | Assessment |
|---|---:|---|
| Revenue Risk Scanner | 10-25% | Human mainly reviews rules, product quality, and edge cases. |
| AI Setup Support | 40-70% | Client context, expectation control, tool choices, and handholding create manual work. |

### Judgment

Revenue Risk Scanner fits the CEO's AI-autonomous goal better.

AI Setup Support can only fit as cashflow if constrained to:

- templates
- checklists
- screen-recorded handoff
- no custom integrations
- no unlimited support
- no "teach me everything" sessions

## 3. AI Sales Potential

| AI Sales Potential Item | Revenue Risk Scanner | AI Setup Support |
|---|---:|---:|
| SEO-only sales | 4 | 3 |
| App Store sales | 5 | 1 |
| API sales | 4 | 1 |
| No-meeting purchase | 5 | 2 |
| Low human sales need | 4 | 2 |
| Total | 22/25 | 9/25 |

### Revenue Risk Scanner

Strong because:

- URL input can show value instantly.
- Shopify App Store path exists.
- API path exists.
- Free scan can create self-serve conversion.
- No meeting is required if output is strong enough.

### AI Setup Support

Weak because:

- Buyers often need trust before purchasing setup help.
- The work feels custom.
- App Store/API channels do not fit naturally.
- Customers may ask many context-specific questions.
- It can easily become a sales-call business.

### Judgment

AI Setup Support is not a strong AI-sales business.

It can sell quickly through direct content, warm network, or small fixed offers, but it does not naturally sell itself through AI/product-led distribution.

## 4. JPY 1M/Month Difficulty

| Business | Difficulty | Example Path |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue Risk Scanner | Medium-high now, medium if app/paid scan works | 200 customers at JPY 5,000/month or 50 customers at JPY 20,000/month |
| AI Setup Support | Medium for owner-led service, high for autonomous model | 10 packages at JPY 100,000/month or 20 packages at JPY 50,000/month |

### Revenue Risk Scanner

Harder at first, but scales better once automated.

### AI Setup Support

JPY 1M/month is possible faster if the owner sells higher-ticket packages.

But that path is human-heavy:

- sales conversations
- onboarding
- client-specific setup
- troubleshooting
- expectation management

If priced low, volume becomes the problem.

If priced high, trust and sales become the problem.

### Judgment

AI Setup Support can reach JPY 1M/month faster only by violating the AI-autonomous principle.

As a fixed low-touch product, JPY 100k-300k/month is more realistic than JPY 1M/month.

## 5. Five-Year Entry Barrier

| Business | 5-Year Barrier | Assessment |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue Risk Scanner | Medium-high if data/integrations compound | Stronger over time through benchmark data, Shopify integration, API, monitoring, and remediation workflows. |
| AI Setup Support | Low unless productized into platform | Generic setup knowledge will commoditize quickly as AI tools improve. |

### Revenue Risk Scanner Barrier

Potential barriers:

- ecommerce risk dataset
- repeated finding patterns
- Shopify/store integrations
- agency/API distribution
- monitoring history
- benchmark comparisons
- remediation templates

### AI Setup Support Barrier

Weak barriers:

- tool setup knowledge gets outdated
- competitors can copy packages
- AI tools will simplify setup
- clients may use built-in onboarding
- no proprietary data unless systemized

Possible barrier only if converted into:

- reusable setup templates
- internal orchestration product
- AI workflow marketplace
- implementation playbooks
- automation scripts
- recurring maintenance subscription

### Judgment

AI Setup Support is a cashflow bridge, not a five-year moat.


## Risk Analysis

### Main Risk

The offer becomes custom consulting.

That would create short-term revenue but damage the main company direction.

### Second Risk

The buyer does not know what they need.

AI setup customers often ask broad questions:

```text
What should I do with AI?
```

That question is dangerous because it turns into strategy consulting.

### Third Risk

The market commoditizes.

By 2027-2028, many tools will provide better onboarding, templates, and integrated agents.

### Fourth Risk

It competes with Revenue Risk Scanner attention.

Every custom client hour is an hour not spent improving finding quality.

## Better Strategic Use

Use AI Setup Support only to produce:

- cash
- internal orchestration templates
- delivery automation patterns
- reusable AI workflow assets
- proof that the company can operate with AI agents

Do not let it become the identity of the company.

## CEO Judgment

```text
CAUTION
```

AI Setup Support is acceptable as a short-term cashflow bridge.

It is not acceptable as the main business.

## Recommended Decision

Proceed only if all constraints are accepted:

1. Fixed package only.
2. No open-ended consulting.
3. Maximum 2 hours human work per customer.
4. Sell with upfront payment.
5. Use delivery templates.
6. Reuse learnings for internal AI operations and Revenue Risk Scanner.
7. Stop or narrow the offer if it delays Revenue Risk Scanner finding-quality work.

## Practical Next Step

Create one fixed offer:

```text
AI Operator Setup Pack
```

Then validate with 5 prospects or one landing page.

Do not build a broad AI consulting business.

Do not call it consulting.

Do not sell custom transformation.
