# AI 2031 Opportunity Map V1

## Purpose

Identify AI-led business opportunities that could plausibly exceed JPY 1B annual revenue by 2031.

Conditions:

- AI is core to the product, not a superficial wrapper.
- Human role is mainly final approval, exception handling, governance, or business direction.
- SaaS/API/platform models are preferred.
- Ideas can be immature in 2026 if the 2031 opportunity is large.

## Market Context

External signals used:

- Gartner forecasts worldwide AI spending of about `$2.52T` in 2026. Source: https://www.gartner.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2026-1-15-gartner-says-worldwide-ai-spending-will-total-2-point-5-trillion-dollars-in-2026
- McKinsey estimated generative AI could create `$2.6T-$4.4T` in annual economic value across use cases. Source: https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/mckinsey-digital/our-insights/the-economic-potential-of-generative-ai-the-next-productivity-frontier
- IDC has projected AI could contribute multi-trillion-dollar economic impact by 2030. Source summary: https://www.axios.com/2024/09/17/ai-global-economy-idc-2030

Interpretation:

The TAM is large enough. The hard part is not whether AI markets grow. The hard part is choosing a wedge with:

- real budget
- workflow lock-in
- data advantage
- measurable ROI
- low human labor
- defensible distribution

## Revenue Risk Scanner Classification

Revenue Risk Scanner is not a standalone "scanner category" if it wants to reach JPY 1B by 2031.

It belongs to:

```text
AI Revenue Operations for Ecommerce
```

Specifically:

```text
Ecommerce Revenue Risk Intelligence
```

Current version:

```text
URL-based diagnostic tool
```

2031-scale version:

```text
Autonomous revenue-risk monitoring and remediation platform for ecommerce.
```

Required evolution:

1. scan public pages
2. score revenue risk
3. monitor changes
4. recommend fixes
5. generate policy/FAQ/product-page updates
6. integrate with Shopify/analytics/reviews/support
7. measure outcomes
8. automate routine remediation with human approval
