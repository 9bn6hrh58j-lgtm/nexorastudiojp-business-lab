# Zero Base 100 Fresh Opportunities V1

## Purpose

Explore completely new business candidates.

All previous candidates are banned.

## Explicitly Banned

Do not use or revive:

- Revenue Risk Scanner
- AI diagnosis products
- AI consulting
- AI outsourcing / proxy-service concepts
- AI setup products
- AI cancellation agent
- AI phone agent
- AI reservation agent
- Cost Finder concepts
- any prior candidate from previous rankings

## Remaining Conditions

Only these conditions remain:

1. Near-zero initial cost
2. First sale within 30 days
3. Repeatable 150,000 JPY/month
4. Future 1,000,000 JPY+ monthly profit
5. Desire intensity
6. Result certainty
7. Explanation-free demand
8. Build vs Buy resistance
9. AI-led operation

## Scoring

Each score is 1 to 5.

| Axis | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Desire | How strongly users want it |
| Certainty | Whether output reliably creates value |
| No-Explain | Whether the offer is instantly understood |
| BvB | Whether it beats generic ChatGPT/Claude/self-build |
| AI-Led | Whether AI can operate most of delivery |
| 30D Sale | Whether first sale within 30 days is plausible |
| 150k | Whether 150,000 JPY/month is repeatable |
| 1M Profit | Whether 1,000,000 JPY/month profit is plausible |

## Executive Judgment

The best fresh opportunities are not generic AI tools.

The strongest new themes are:

1. emotionally recurring consumer utilities
2. family and household coordination utilities
3. hobby workflow products with repeat use
4. niche safety / preparedness utilities
5. small physical-world workflow tools that do not require owning operations

The strongest candidates in this fresh scan are:

1. AI Family Memory Capsule
2. AI Pet Care Routine Board

These are not guaranteed winners.

They are fresh candidates that better match:

low start cost

smartphone friendliness
clear output
non-diagnostic value

## Top 10 Fresh Candidates

## 1. AI Home Command Center

Why it is strong:

- recurring household utility
- clear value
- smartphone-friendly
- combines reminders, manuals, warranties, calendar, and checklists
- can start as paid templates/forms before software

Main risk:

- scope can become too broad
- must start with one concrete module

Best first wedge:

```text
manuals + warranties + household reminders
```

## 2. AI Pet Care Routine Board

Why it is strong:

- repeated daily use
- high emotional desire
- clear output
- works on smartphone
- does not require heavy integrations

Main risk:

- consumer pricing may be low

pet sitter brief + daily care board
