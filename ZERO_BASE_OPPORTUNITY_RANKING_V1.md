# Zero Base Opportunity Ranking V1

## Purpose

Remove the assumption that Revenue Risk Scanner is a priority candidate.

From this point, every business opportunity must be evaluated from zero.

Revenue Risk Scanner is included only as one candidate.

It receives no priority bonus.

## Evaluation Axes

1. Desired before explanation
2. Customer acquisition difficulty
3. Can create 150,000 JPY within 30 days
4. AI-led operation potential
5. Can scale beyond 1,000,000 JPY per month
6. Build vs Buy resistance
7. Desire intensity

Scoring:

```text
5 = strong
4 = good
3 = mixed
2 = weak
1 = poor
```

For customer acquisition difficulty:

```text
5 = easy
1 = very hard
```

## Important Assumption Change

Previous priority:

```text
Revenue Risk Scanner as Tier1
```

New priority:

```text
No protected Tier1.
```

The company should prioritize the opportunity with the strongest combination of:

```text
immediate human desire
fast cash potential
AI-led operation
scalability
defensibility
```

## Executive Judgment

The strongest near-term candidates are not generic AI SaaS tools.

They are fixed-scope products that remove urgent, annoying, money-related, or reputation-related work.

Top five zero-base candidates:

1. B2B Urgent Response Assistant Pack
2. Ecommerce Policy Fix Pack
3. AI Operator Setup Pack
4. Cost / Overpayment Finder
5. Urgent Paperwork / Claim Assistant

Revenue Risk Scanner ranks lower because:

- the pain is not obvious before explanation
- ChatGPT can imitate surface-level analysis
- customer access is harder
- 30-day 150,000 JPY revenue path is weaker
- payment intent is not yet validated

This does not kill Revenue Risk Scanner.

It removes its priority protection.

## Adjusted Ranking Logic

The total score alone is not enough.

Some ideas score high but have hidden execution difficulty.

For example:

- cost finders are attractive, but may need data access
- emergency service marketplaces are attractive, but need provider networks
- contract checks have strong pain, but legal trust boundaries matter
- subsidy finders have strong desire, but rules change and accuracy matters

Therefore, the final ranking weights:

1. speed to first cash
2. low operational burden
3. AI-led delivery
4. strong human desire
5. realistic founder execution

## Strategic Decision

```text

```


```text

-> repeated pain discovery
-> SaaS/API only after demand is proven
```
