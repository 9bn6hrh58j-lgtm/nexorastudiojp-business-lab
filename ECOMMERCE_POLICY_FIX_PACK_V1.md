# Ecommerce Policy Fix Pack V1

## Purpose

Define the short-term cashflow product:

```text
Ecommerce Policy Fix Pack
```

Goal:

```text
Generate revenue within 90 days without turning into custom consulting.
```

This is a fixed product, not a consulting service.

## Operating Rules

- Human work is final review only.
- No open-ended strategy calls.
- No custom legal advice.
- No tax/customs/import compliance advice.
- No unlimited revisions.
- Customer input must be form-based.
- Delivery must use templates.
- Output must feed reusable knowledge into Revenue Risk Scanner.
- Operable on MacBook Pro 2017.

## Product Definition

Ecommerce Policy Fix Pack improves buyer-facing policy clarity for ecommerce stores.

It helps merchants reduce buyer hesitation caused by unclear:

- shipping policy
- returns/refunds policy
- duties/taxes wording
- delivery damage handling
- support expectations
- FAQ snippets

Positioning:

```text
Fix policy clarity gaps that may cause buyers to hesitate before purchase.
```

Avoid:

```text
Legal compliance review
Custom consulting
Market entry strategy
Guaranteed conversion lift
```

## 1. Product Specification

### Target Customer

Best fit:

- Shopify / ecommerce store owner
- DTC founder
- small ecommerce team
- store already selling or preparing to sell internationally
- merchant has unclear shipping/returns/duties/support copy

Bad fit:

- enterprise legal/compliance team
- regulated products needing legal/import advice
- customers asking for strategy calls
- customers needing exact tax/customs classification

### What Customer Gets

Fixed deliverables:

1. Policy Clarity Score
2. shipping policy rewrite
3. returns/refunds rewrite
4. duties/taxes wording block
5. delivery damage wording block
6. support expectations wording block
7. 5-10 FAQ snippets
8. before/after notes
9. implementation checklist

### Scope Limit

The pack covers buyer-facing clarity only.

It does not cover:

- legal compliance
- tax advice
- customs advice
- import eligibility
- full website audit
- brand strategy
- ongoing support
- live implementation inside Shopify

### Form Rule

If a customer leaves optional fields blank, proceed anyway.

Do not ask follow-up questions unless:

- URL is broken
- policy pages are missing
- product category is legally sensitive
- customer asks for legal/tax/customs advice

## 5. AI Generation Workflow

### Workflow

```text
1. Customer submits form.
2. AI extracts policy pages and product context.
3. AI identifies buyer-facing clarity gaps.
4. AI generates fixed policy copy using templates.
5. AI generates FAQ snippets.
6. AI generates implementation checklist.
7. Human performs final review.
8. PDF is delivered.
9. Reusable rules/templates are logged for Revenue Risk Scanner.
```

### AI Prompt Structure

```text
Role:
You are an ecommerce buyer-confidence copy assistant.

Goal:
Rewrite policy sections for clarity and buyer reassurance.

Rules:
- Do not provide legal, tax, customs, regulatory, or compliance advice.
- Do not make guarantees.
- Keep wording practical and merchant-editable.
- Use the customer's store context.
- If information is missing, use bracketed placeholders.

Inputs:
- Store URL
- Store country
- Target countries
- Product category
- Current shipping policy
- Current return/refund policy
- Current contact/support info
- Current FAQ if available

Output:
- summary
- policy clarity score
- shipping rewrite
- returns/refunds rewrite
- duties/taxes wording block
- delivery damage wording block
- support expectations block
- FAQ snippets
- implementation checklist
```

### Template Rule

AI output must fit the delivery template.

No custom sections unless approved by human.
