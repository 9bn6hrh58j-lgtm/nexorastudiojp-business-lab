## Why This Exists

Many businesses think Japan launch starts with translation.

In practice, translation is often not the first problem.

Japanese customers may hesitate because they cannot quickly understand:

- whether the business is trustworthy
- whether the product is safe or suitable
- how shipping works
- whether extra duties or fees apply
- what happens if there is a problem
- whether support will be reliable

This audit is designed to surface those issues early.
