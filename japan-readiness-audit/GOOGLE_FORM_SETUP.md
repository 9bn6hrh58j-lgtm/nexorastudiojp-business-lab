## 9. Fit Decision Rules

### Fit

Use Fit when:

- the website is live
- the business sells online
- Japan is current or plausible
- the category is not dominated by legal/regulatory complexity
- a website readiness review can produce useful feedback

### Maybe

Use Maybe when:

- Japan intent is unclear
- shipping status is unclear
- the website is live but category fit is uncertain
- one follow-up question can clarify fit

### Not Fit

Use Not Fit when:

- there is no live website
- the business is Japan-only already
- the request is legal, tax, customs, import, medical, or regulatory
- the applicant expects guaranteed sales
- the website requires login to review
