# Japan Readiness Audit - Target 10 Companies

## Objective

Finalize the first 10 companies for LinkedIn outreach.

The goal is to find the first paid Founder Beta audit customer.

This is not market research.  
This is not new product development.  
This is outreach preparation only.

## CEO Decisions Applied

- Notification email: use a temporary email the CEO can reliably check; change later if needed.
- Payment flow: send the Gumroad payment link only after Fit review.
- Founder Beta scarcity copy: start with `10 Slots Available`.
- Payment guidance language: English only.

## Selection Criteria

Companies were selected using the following filters:

1. Overseas business with a live ecommerce website.
2. Product category has plausible Japan appeal.
3. Public website shows Japan shipping, international shipping, or cross-border readiness.
4. Website has likely trust, FAQ, shipping, returns, customs, or localization review needs.
5. Company appears reachable through founder, ecommerce, growth, or customer support channels.
6. Avoid categories where legal, import, medical, food safety, or regulatory complexity dominates the first conversation.

## Exclusion Notes

Do not prioritize:

- very large beauty brands with internal localization teams
- Japanese companies selling from Japan
- marketplaces without a clear owner/operator contact
- heavily regulated medical, supplement, food safety, or import-sensitive categories
- brands that already have a complete Japanese-localized experience

## First DM Structure

Use one personalized issue per company.

Format:

```text
Hi [Name], I came across [Company] and noticed you already support Japan / international shipping.

One thing that may matter for Japanese buyers: [specific observation].

I am running the first 10 Founder Beta Japan Readiness Audits for small brands. It reviews trust, localization, FAQ, payment, shipping, return, and buyer hesitation issues before a Japan launch.

Would you like me to send the sample report?
```
