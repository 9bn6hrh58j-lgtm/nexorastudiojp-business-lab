Recommended:

```text

```

- KPI is only 1 reply.

### If They Ask What The Audit Includes

Send:

```text
It is a written website audit from a Japanese buyer-readiness perspective.

It reviews trust signals, localization gaps, FAQ gaps, payment/shipping/return clarity, and buyer hesitation points.

It is not legal, tax, customs, regulatory, or sales-guarantee advice.

I can send the sample report if useful.
```

### If They Ask About Price

Send:

```text
Founder Beta is $49 for the first 10 slots.

I review fit first, then send the payment link if the site is a good match.

The written audit is delivered within 3-5 business days after payment.
```

### If They Say Japan Is Not A Priority

Send:

```text
Totally understood.

If Japan becomes active later, I would start by checking whether the site clearly answers shipping, duties, returns, support, and trust questions for Japanese buyers before spending on translation or ads.

Happy to reconnect when Japan is closer on your roadmap.
```

## Daily Review

After sending the first 5 DMs, check once daily:

- new replies
- connection accepts
- sample report requests
- form responses
- objections

Do not rewrite the entire strategy before at least 5 DMs have been sent.

## Decision Rules

### If 1 reply arrives

Mark KPI as achieved and move to reply handling.

### If 1 form response arrives

Review Fit / Maybe / Not Fit.

If Fit:

- send Gumroad payment link if ready
- if Gumroad is not ready, tell the prospect the payment link will follow shortly

### If zero replies after 5 DMs and 5 days

Do not change target market yet.

First adjust:

1. shorten first DM
2. remove Founder Beta mention
3. make the opening observation more specific
4. test next 5 companies from `TARGET_10_COMPANIES.md`
